2019-08-15		Firmware v1.32 for Windows & MacOS
1. Fixed the latency issue when using with Switch Joy-cons attached. 
2. Support USB connection on Switch mode.
3. Enhance the accuracy of joystick.

----------------------------------------------------------

Firmware update instruction
1. Press and hold L1+R1+START buttons on the controller to put it on its update mode. LED on the top will blink in red.
2. Connect the controller to your Windows or macOS via the USB cable. 
3. Click on "USB Upgrade" on your device, search for firmware.dat in the pop-up window and run it;  
4. Un-plug the gamepad when the upgrade process is done, then restart it before using.
